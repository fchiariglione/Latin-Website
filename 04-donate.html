Giving Access to Opportunities

Latino U College Access is committed to providing consistent support and guidance to Scholars on their journeys to and through college and into thriving careers. We work tirelessly to change the narrative of educational and social inequity in America. Your generosity empowers Scholars to reach higher than they ever imagined possible.

$5,000 Guides one Scholar through the entire college admissions process.
$2,500 Provides one-on-one SAT and ACT preparation for two Latino U Scholars.
$1,000 Ensures a smooth transition from high school into college for an entire Latino U Scholars cohort.
$500 Supports a career-readiness workshop to prepare Scholars for entering the workforce as first-generation graduates.
